HAMBURGER OF THE WEEK
Time
Level: Easy Total: 25 min.
Prep: 10 min. Cook:15 min.
Yield: 4 Hamburgers
BENGAL HAMBURGER
26 Reviews
"No more dry, lackluster burgers. These are juicy, and spices can be easily added or changed to suit anyone's taste. Baste frequently with your favorite barbeque sauce. If you find the meat mixture too mushy, just add more bread crumbs until it forms patties that hold thier shape."
Ingredients
  • 24 onces ground chuck
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Spice Mix, optional, recipe follows
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 4 slices cheese, such as Cheddar, American Cheese
  • 4 soft hamberger-style buns, split
  • 4 slices beefsteak tomatoes, optional
  • Assorted lettuces and green, such as iceberg
  • Assorted mustards, optional
  • Mayonnaise, optional
  • Ketchup, optional
Spice Mix
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire suace
  • 2 tablespoons garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons onion powder
  • Hot Sauce, to taste
Instructions
  1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F. Place a roasting rack on a foil-lined baking sheet in the oven
  2. Using your hands, break the meat into small pieces and combine evenly but loosely on a parchment or waxed paper-lined baking sheet. Spread the meat out and season it generously with salt and pepper. If desired, add the spice mix at this time.
  3. Divide the meat into 4 portions (about 6 ounces each). Using your hands, form each portion into a ball-shape by gently tosing it from 1 hand to the other. (Don't over work or press too firmlyon the meat.) Gently form each portion into a patty about 3 1/2 inches wide and 1-inch-thick
  4. Preheat a large cast iron skillet over medium-low heat for 5 minutes.Raise the heat to high and add the oil. Add the patties and cook, turing once, until well-browned, about 2 minutes each side.
  5. Using a spatula, transfer the hambergers to the roasting rack in the oven and continue cooking to desired doneness, 8 to 9 minutes for medium-rare, 10 to 11 minutes for medium, and 13 to 15 minutes for well-done. If you are using the cheese, top the hambergers during thier last couple minutes of cooking to melt.
  6. Transfer the hambergers to plate, let rest for a couple minutes before serving. Meanwhile, toast the hamberger buns. Assemble the hambergers with the condiments and toppings of your choice. Serve.
  7. When cooking on a countertop grill, turn on grill, then place burgers on the heated grill.

    8. Time for a countertop grill:
  8. For Medium-Rare: Cook for 3 minutes covered and then unplug the machine and continue to cook covered for 2 to 3 more minutes.
  9. For Medium: Cook for 4 mimutes covered and then unplug the machine and continue to cook covered for 4 more minutes.
  10. For Well: Cook for 5 minutes covered and then unplug the machine.
